Experience

this is what I've done

While I have worked many jobs in my lifetime, I have chosen to hone in on the 2 experiences I had that have best prepared me for the career I am seeking.

The first experience I will discuss is that of working as a research assistant. As a research assistant I worked for Dr. Jacob Rugh and helped with data input. My job specifically was to find out if people who were convicted of storming the capital served in the military, went to college, and had a job.

The second experience is when I got to work as an exposure therapy assistant. Within this position I was in charge of creating experiences that pushed a 12 year old girl with severe OCD in the form of germaphobia to face some of her fears. In working with this girl I was able to better understand the thought processes of those who are germaphobic and see how disasterous the condition to be anyone's life no matter their age.
